OCTOBER 28, 2017, KIIT UNIVERSITY

AVISHKAR

Platform for undergraduate students to showcase their work on their respective research papers which helps in solving real life problems using applications of Computer Science and Information Technology.

Avishkar is an initiative by CINE, which develops the innovative spirit amongst students and inspires them to take up research and development of their brain child. It provides a platform for undergraduate students to showcase their work on their respective research papers which helps in solving real life problems using applications of Computer Science and Information Technology.

Important Notes

Target Audience

Aspirant can be any undergraduate student irrespective of their streams.

Eligibility

A team must comprise of not more than 3 members (2 students + 1 advisor/mentor/guide).
Students can be undergraduates from any stream or +2 students .
Each team must have an advisor/mentor/guide.
Members of a team must be affiliated with the same educational institute.
The project has to be recommended by the head of the institute/Department.
The official language of the contest is English. All written and spoken contest materials will be in English.

Fees

The total fees charged from the selected teams will be 1500 in which they will be allowed to attend the CINE conference (Includes kit, breakfast, lunch and snacks).

How To Apply ?

SUBMISSION

Soft copy of the project template must be submitted by Emailing us at cine2017.sce@kiit.ac.in
Documentation part of the project should be prepared as per the template provided in the website.
Only selected project will be called for the presentation and that will be intimated through their provided email id.

Contestants need to meet all of the following requirements at the time of the final contest presentations:

Be enrolled as a student at an accredited educational institution.

Not already be enrolled in another team with a different project.

Attractions

Selected projects will be tentatively published in Special Edition of CSI magazine.
Contestants will be judged by special jury from industry.
Presence of Angel Investors who would be willing to sponsor the Appealing Projects.
Kit will be provided to each team along with breakfast, lunch and snacks.
Projects with High market value will be eligible for Incubation.
